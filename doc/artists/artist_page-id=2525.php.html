JAH LEVI & the Higher Reasoning - Artist Page
JAH LEVI & the Higher Reasoning
Discography  ·  Interview  ·  Booking Agency  ·  Similar Music
Biography:
 

Originally from Philadelphia, Jah Levi, the son of record producer and world renowned folklorist Kenneth S. Goldstein, first learned guitar from the late Rev. Gary Davis in the 1960's, and later learned bass guitar as his "main" instrument. After studying music with many great master musicians and teachers, he majored in Ethnomusicology and world music studies. A trained herbalist and acupuncturist, he is "livicated" to proliferating consciousness through proper living "standing up to do the right thing."

Jah Levi and the Higher Reasoning, are world class musicians who are versatile enough to spontaneously perform all genres of music. His ability as a band leader is exemplified by the trust and loyalty of his group, several of whom have been with him since the group's beginning and many who return (from the far corners of the globe) to perform year after year. They perform a unique blend of Reggae-Rastafarian music that is spiritually uplifting and fun to dance to. Jah Levi founded Theocratic Records, one of the first independent reggae record labels in the United States of America, and Jah works Studios, the first solar powered digital (24-bit) recording studio in the world.

Jah Levi has performed on the same stage with many reggae, folk and pop super stars. He has enjoyed chart action with his music on radio and television around the world. He has over 20 albums out, and 25 years of touring and performing his unique blend of Reggae / world fusion worldwide. Jah Levi is well known for his abilities as a band leader, and is an independent music producer and engineer as well. His music continues to influence people with a powerful synergy of uplifting music and thought provoking lyrics, truly medicine for the hungry and afflicted soul, spreading a message of love and unity for all beings.

Jah Levi is an ordained bishop of the Essene church, a minister of the soga del alma church of Peru and a card carrying member of the Native American church. He is the founder of The Healing Light Alternative Medical Practice in the Netherlands, and practices a unique form of ceremonial shamanism and healing work with plant medicines. He is known worldwide as an adept facilitator of transformation and healing. He has influenced a whole generation of musicians, and many inspired and successful bands were born from musical and spiritual seeds planted in the hearts of many though his music. His moving performances have become milestones in the lives of those who experience him in concert.

He is not only inspired, but educated, holding advanced degrees in ethnomusicology, electronics, engineering, medicine and martial arts as well as certification as a master of Luthiery (musical instrument building) with in depth knowledge of over 200 stringed and percussive instruments. He is an adept musician of the highest order. His conviction to the Rastafarian faith, combined with his unique understanding of world music, music production, healing arts, technology and spirituality, comprise a man on a mission: to deliver the message of love, unity, and survival to millions of people worldwide through live performances, recordings and radio broadcasts in over 72 nations.

Interview:
 
From One Love Lifestyles, Jah Levi

Excerpts from an interview by Laura Barnum, September, 2004 [reproduced by courtesy of the artist]

Just a taste of Jah Levi?s depth, flowing out in softly-spoken, powerful words?

I am a Rastafarian in Transformation. I represent the Essene lineage. I am a vegetarian and I work with music and medicine for the healing of the nation. And if you are to come up with a single job title for what I do, I am a Change Agent; I facilitate change, and to facilitate change, you have to become change. Basically, I feel that whatever it is you want to do in this life, you have to become it. Like if you want to bring peace into this world, then you have to become peace. If you want to bring light into this world, then you have to be light. And for me, I want to bring change into this world, so I have to change...

When I go out to play music ? whether it?s an audience or even around a campfire ? it?s all alchemy to me, it?s all vibrational medicine. So you go into a circle of people, and everyone has a certain frequency that they vibrate at in their being, and so you see this collection of beings in this circle, and everyone has their tuning, an attunement, and collectively we make a chord, we resonate sympathetically to each other, and sum is the Frequency of our make. That?s why they say the sum of the whole is greater than it?s parts. We say that the Spirit is present, but the Spirit is the vibration, us vibrating as one. I guess you could say it?s a very important aspect of what ?One Love? means to me. Like if people say to me, ?what does one love lifestyle mean to you?? Like when people ask, ?Jah Levi, what is love to you?? It all comes down to love. We all came from love and in the end, to love we must return.

We talk about one God, but it is really the totality of what we believe. If God were already here, we would know that we are one... We forget that we are one here, so we create lifestyles, we create lifestyles that help us remember that we are one. I feel that oneness with all beings. I guess that?s where it came from originally, this knowingness that we are all one, and anytime I saw anything that separated us, I knew that that was not the way, and I had to find the way, and I found that the plants helped me find the way...

I always had this vision to bring music, which is medicine, healing to the people, and I, 25 years ago, I shopped it around to all the record lables, because there were only major record labels at the time?there were no independent record labels at the time?I shopped it to all of them, and they said, ?your music is nice, but it?s too spiritual, and we don?t think people are going to buy it.? So I had no choice but to start my own label....The message that I was singing about then, that I had to convince everybody of,... now people are open and ready to receive it, and thank God I?m not too old to do it. I?m really inspired right now, to help facilitate this change that I speak of....

I don?t see being a ?Rastaman? as being a choice. It chooses you. So when you are born, you are born a Rastafarian. You eventually wake up to that, you realize that; it?s something that you are. Because it?s not a religion; it?s a way of life... I also recognize that this man Haile Selassie, he does fulfill prophecy in terms of the emanation of God on Earth in this time. I do believe in the coming of Jehshua as God, this so called Jesus Christ. I reject the name Jesus Christ, because that was the name of the first slave ship to bring slaves from Africa to the New World. It was the S.S. Jesus and Mary of Lubeck. I reject that name; that?s part of the programming. The name Jesus didn?t even exist at the time. His name was Jehshua, and so I recognize him coming and the significance of his teachings, and what I extracted from those teachings were two things that influenced me greatly. One of those was vegetarianism and raw foods, and the other is the golden rule, what we call the ?golden rule.? Do unto others as you would have them do unto you. ...? if you can live by these two laws, then you will automatically be living by all other laws.? Do Unto Others as You Would Have Them Do Unto You and Love the Lord Your God Above All Things. Love, love, above all things.

So I see Haile Selassie fulfilled the prophecy of how he said he would come again through the same lineage of David, not as a lamb to be slaughtered, but this time he would come as a lion to conquering and to conquer.....
