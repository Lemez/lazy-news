Diliup Gabadamudalige - Artist Page
Diliup Gabadamudalige
Discography  ·  Booking Agency  ·  Similar Music
Biography:
 

Diliup Gabadamudalige studied Piano at Trinity College of Music in London under Mrs. Manel Weerasinge. He also studied violin at Royal School of Music under Mrs. Eileen Prins.

He is a member of the National Music Council and a  visiting lecturer of the Kelaniya and Katubedde Universities delivering lectures on Computer Music Technology. The syllabus for this course was developed by him.

Gabadamudalige introduced many artists to the Sri Lankan Music industry, including Rukshan Dole (Rap Squad currently in USA), Priyantha Perera (currently in Japan), Steve de Silva (son of Desmond de Silva), Bathiya Jayakody (Bathiya & Santush), Ranidu Lankage (Ranidu & Ashanthi), Yasmin de Silva (currently with Sohan and the Xperiments), Monique Wille (currently with Ultimate), Nevanthi Peires (originally a backup singer with Bathiya & Santush, joined Diliup and won the Golden Clef Most Promising Singer of the Year Award 2002 soon after), Wathsala Gunatilleke (winner of the Showboat Karaoke Championship 2002. Currently training on the job with Diliup).

Diliup was Sri Lanka's first true one man Band playing all music live with keyboards and MIDI pedal bass. He was the Music Director of Young Asia Television (Ya TV) from its inception till December 2003. Diliup pioneered the Institute of Computer Music Technology in Sri Lanka in 2002. Several students who graduated have opened their studios in other parts of the country.

Diliup is the recipient of many awards: First place in South East Asia for Trinity College of Music London Grade in piano, Sri Lanka Cancer Society Appreciation Award for Fund Raising Concerts, 1st place at Selections in Sri Lanka for World Popular Song Festival held in Tokyo, Japan. 1987 (original composition); Island Music Award for Duo of the Year 1992; Golden Clef Song of the Year Award 1992. (composition); Golden Clef Instrumental of the Year Award 1992. (composition); Golden Clef Best Duo Award 1993; Golden Clef Soloist of the Year Award 1993; Golden Clef Best Duo Award 1994; Presidential Film Award 2000 Best Film Music Director (Film Rajya Sevaya Pinisai); Presidential Film Award 2000 Best Song Composer (Sanda Thaniyama sung by Nirosha Virajini); Golden clef Musician of the Year Award 2003.

Diliup has composed songs for Nirosha Virajini, Samitha Mudunkotuwa, Bathiya & Santhush, Nissanka Diddeniya, Mariazelle, Nelu Adhikari, Dalrene, Manila Perera, Ramona Carr (USA), Maxi Rozairo, Downtown, Urban Sounds, Rap Squad.

Diliup did the very first Tsunami Concert in Sri Lanka called "Reachout" on the 28th. of January, which featured many of the top Sri Lankan artists. This was a sellout concert. Due to its success he is holding "Reachout 2" on the 25th. of February, this time featuring hip hop, soft rock and pop artists only.
